Exploring Malaysia's Tourism Recovery: Where Are Travellers Going and Why?

This visualisation explores how international tourism in Malaysia has recovered, which countries visitors are coming from, and which states and entry points are shaping the new tourism landscape.

FIT2179 Data Visualisation 2

Created by Hanzala Aman

1. Welcome Back, Travellers

Malaysia's tourism industry has gone through major disruption and recovery. This section will show the overall trend in visitor arrivals over time.

2. Where Are Malaysia's Visitors Coming From?

Different countries contribute differently to Malaysia's tourism recovery. This section will compare the largest visitor source countries.

3. Malaysia's Tourism Gateways

Visitors do not enter Malaysia in the same way. Airports, land checkpoints, and sea ports each play different roles in the recovery story.

4. Which States Benefit Most?

Tourism recovery is not equal across Malaysia. Some states receive much higher visitor traffic because of airports, borders, and popular destinations.

5. Tourism Recovery Is Uneven

Recovery patterns differ by state and entry point, showing that Malaysia's tourism comeback is not happening at the same speed everywhere.

6. Who Is Travelling?

The dataset also allows us to compare male and female arrivals across countries, states, and time.

7. Malaysia's New Tourism Landscape

The final section will summarise the key findings and show how Malaysia's tourism recovery is shaped by geography, entry points, and visitor origin.
